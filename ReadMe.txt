=======================================================================
				Legend
=======================================================================
P: Punch
K: Kick

U: Up
D: Down
L: Left
R:Right

QCF: Quater circle Forward; D, DF, F (A Hadouken motion)

dp: Dragon Punch; F, D, DF (A Dragon Punch motion...duh :P) 

HCF: Half Circle Forward; F, DF, D, DB, B (A Shakunetsu Hadouken motion)


---------------
System
===============
Dodge: LP+LK

Dodge Attack: Press either punch or kick during dodge recovery

Roll: F+LP+LK

Parry: Tap F immediately before being hit (Can be done in the air). Press D for low parry 

---------------
Specials - Use either 2 Punches or Kicks for their EX versions (requires at least 1/2 Power Bar)
===============
Tiger Shot: QCF + P

Ground Tiger Shot: QCF + K

Tiger Uppercut: DP + P

Tiger Crush: DP + K

----------------
Super Arts - Use either 2 Punches or Kicks for their max versions (requires 3 Power Bars)
================
Tiger Cannon: QCFx2 + P

Ground Tiger Cannon: QCBx2 + P

Tiger Genocide: QCFx2 + K

Tiger Raid: QCBx2 + K
